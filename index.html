Why QSBench?

Most quantum datasets are too small, lack paired ideal/noisy targets, or are difficult to reproduce consistently. QSBench addresses these issues by providing high-quality, richly annotated, and fully reproducible synthetic datasets specifically designed for Quantum Machine Learning and noise robustness research.
