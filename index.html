QSBench

Ideal. Noisy. Reproducible.

QSBench is a family of high-quality synthetic quantum datasets designed for Quantum Machine Learning, benchmarking, and hybrid quantum-classical research.

Each dataset includes paired ideal and noisy expectation values, rich circuit metadata, structural metrics, and deterministic train/validation/test splits.

We create reproducible, well-documented quantum datasets so researchers can reliably test models, study the effects of noise, and compare results across experiments.
